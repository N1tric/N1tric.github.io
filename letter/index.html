To Wang Sidan,

Happy birthday!

You know I’m not good at writing. So you may wanna consider this as shits or something.

I’m really grateful for what you have done to help me. You are such a sweet girl. Things you did I could never repay off. However, you seem to have so many friends, I dont think there are things I will be able to help.

Chatting with you must be very enjoyable, I think. Though it’s a pity that I’m not good at chatting and I cant feel any happier after chatting with you. I’m sorry that we become friends. I’m not good at being a friend. Maybe you’re feeling terrible about this letter, but I hope it could somewhat change your opinion of being friends with me, or give you another way of thinking about friendship.

I remember that our first conversation is on the Internet, which I wanted to correct your mistake caused by me in that English class. And the fun part of it is that I now realize this seems to be the most common way that I make friends in the real world, though now I dont make any new friends in reality anymore. I’m trying to avoid new friends, yet my old friends are going to leave me one after another.

Oh that’s kind of off-topic. What I really want to mean by, is that YOU and someone else in Grade 8 would be my last heterosexual friends in the next few years. You guys are now the only ones that temporarily consider me as “oh this guy is so far not bad”. But after time, you will leave me again as anyone else did.

I thought about words you said before, and I gonna tell you what friendships really consist of. It’s not benefit-free that they need. The friendship, is consist of, dependency and interests. You may look up the word “interests” because it is not of the common meaning. It’s like, if you need me to be my teammate in games, then we are friends; if you need my help to do anything, we are friends. As you can see, there is no dependency or interest between us. This is an indication that our friendship would not maintain for long.

Honestly saying, I’m tired of chatting a whole day long just for maintaining our goddamn friendship. And I also don’t need your birthday gifts. I just really really hate that shit. It gives me a reason to stay being friends. I like to be free, not be binding with each other by the fucking gifts.

I can’t give you anything like anybody else. I can’t give you happiness. All the things I can give is infinite sadness and hopelessness. I don’t wish you to be my friend. You are not supposed to bear this.

And at last, DO NOT GIVE me any gifts. I didn’t spend a lot of time writing this letter, and I completely didn’t spend much on you. I hope you do the same as I do.

Take care,
Nutr1t07

¶后记

我原想好好地写一篇充满正能量的纯粹祝你生日快乐的信，但是写着写着我就发现我根本没那个能力。有时候我甚至怀疑我自己是不是总是在用充满负能量的语言来博人关注，显得我自己很特殊；我试着避免处处传递负能量，但最后发现我自己实在太敏感了，一与人交流就会陷入失望之中。我非常不喜欢这种情况，我觉得我自己应该避免社交，以脱离痛苦的源泉，但我实在无法做到。情绪是具感染性的，我不觉得你理应承受和我同样的感受。所以请你不要再来烦我了。我不喜欢这样。
